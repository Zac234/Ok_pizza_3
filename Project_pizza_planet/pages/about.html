History

Now I know we are a pizza place but our logo and likeness has been featured prominently in Pixar movies ever since the firt Toy Story movie. Ever since our founding in 1990 by John D., we have been striving to make the perfect customer experience both inside and out. We have over 40 locations across North America and are looking to expand into the European market within 5 years, so needless to say we know how to make a great pizza. Our recipies haven't gone through all the changes those other guys have, we are still using farm fresh ingredients that make the pizza and all the other things we serve taste fresh enough you'd believe that they would have come from the farm itself. Of course Pizza isn't the only thing we sell here, we have a fantastic indoor arcade fun for both the kids and the kids at heart making sure that everyone has a great time!
